Are Video Games Good for You and Your Brain?

Published on March 24, 2025

Image credit: @edtechmagazine.com

Video games have become an integral part of modern entertainment, particularly for teens. While there are benefits, such as improved cognitive skills and social connections in some contexts, the negative effects on mental health cannot be ignored.

Conclusion

While video games can offer certain cognitive and social benefits, it is essential to be aware of their potential negative effects on mental health.
